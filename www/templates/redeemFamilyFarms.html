About Family Farms Creamery


Pittsburgh Ice Cream Company has been churning specialty Farm-to-Cone ice creams and sorbets since 2014. Pittsburgh's food landscape is woven of historic farms and new artisan food producers. Working closely with both, we create new flavors with a timeless taste. With a passion for the future and a deep respect for our past, we handcraft ice cream that brings you the tastes of this place, our place: Pittsburgh.
