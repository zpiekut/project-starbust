Illegal dump sites around the Pittsburgh area are harmful to the environment. Enter the Allegheny Cleanways DumpBusters! Made up of only a handful of volunteers (anywhere from 1-8 people on any given day), the DumpBusters crew tackles illegal dumps of all sizes and remove an average of 900-1200 lbs of debris daily.
